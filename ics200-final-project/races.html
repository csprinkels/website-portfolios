Project D: Races

Takumi Fujiwara vs Shinji Inui

Takumi passes Shinji using a blind attack, but overrevs his engine after ending his attack, blowing his engine for the second time. Despite this, Takumi manages to cross the finish line first, and it is considered a victory

Takumi Fujiwara vs Dr. Toshiya Joshima (God Arm)

Takumi breaks his suspension but is able to pass Joshima when he pulls over to vomit. Takumi believes he lost this race, something Ryosuke and Joshima agree with.

Keisuke Takahashi vs Go Hojo

Go Hojo loses all his grip by entering corners too fast, spins out while Keisuke sprints away

Keisuke Takahashi vs Kozo Hoshino (God Foot)

Even though the race should have continued, Kozo forfeights as he doesn't have any remaining grip in his tires to enter a third round
